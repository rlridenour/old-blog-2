God of Love,

When I see a stranger,  
do you see a beloved child?

When I see an enemy,  
do you see a friend?

May I see what is,  
and not what I fear might be.

Even more,  
may I see what could be,  
if I were to give even a fraction  
of the grace that I have received.

*Amen*
