title: Poem for my Valentine

Some people tell me  
that I look through  
rose colored glasses,  
because life can't  
be that good.

All I know is that  
if my glass is only  
half full it's  
because I have  
drunk deeply  
from the  
joy,  
wonder,  
and  
love  
that is you.
