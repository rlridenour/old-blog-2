A woman asked Jesus  
to heal her daughter,  
and Jesus said nothing.

Pilate asked Jesus  
to defend himself,  
and Jesus said nothing.

Yet the girl was healed,  
and Jesus went to the cross.

Forgive me, Lord,  
for the sin of  
saying everything but  
doing nothing.

*Amen*
